The Usumu festival is essentially Delta’s Got Talent. The celebration takes place in a pair of week long events.

The initial event, the Usumu festival, is a local competition where each city selects its best competitors and is judged by the local Priestesses. It takes place the 3rd week of Grandlen. Agents frequently go out to the countryside and recruit for the initial Usumu event, allowing more rural areas to participate.

The second event, Usumu-di, is where the victors from the first round head to Semara where Sarru Zalalahara judges the performance (or more often, another member of the Zalalahara Dynasty does it in his name). It takes place in the 3rd week of Iannas. The winner gets to perform privately for Sarru Zalalahara and a sponsorship from the Temple of the Twin Goddesses. 

> [!translation] Usumu is Sumerian for Strong + Sing, and Usumu-di is Sumerian for Strong+Sing+Verdict
