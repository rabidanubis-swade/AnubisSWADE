## Heirs of Honor
At the end of the [[Age of Divinity]],  sacrificed himself to rescue Ianna, Goddess of Love and War. The survivors of Honor's divine kingdom in Iasfia sailed to Avaron, conquering the continent and founding The Kurtainian Empire. The Kurtainians were (supposedly) heroic, chivalric, honorable, and just to those they conquered, their rule a Golden Age while the rest of the world suffered the atrocities of the [[Age of Collapse]]. 

This ‘Golden Age’ eventually came to an end. A variety of stresses, ranging from super powerful dragons, to corruption, to bad crop harvests agitated political and economic divisions within the Empire, leading to a collapse. The nail in the coffin was the death of the final emperor without an heir, which led to the [[Kurtainian Empire]] disappearing at the end of the [[Age of Collapse]].

What remained at the dawn of the Age of Heroes was a series of Highprinces that warred with each other perpetually, each claiming to be the proper heir of the Empire. In time however, regions began to divide into subcultures - due to the influence of pre-imperial cultures, the abandonment of imperial ideals, and the simple passage of time.
