Deltan women who wish to get married and find doing so domestically difficult (or undesirable) can sign up with merchants to go abroad in search of a spouse, typically meeting them in a series of dates. Those doing so can either pay up front or put themselves up as collateral in the case they are unsuccessful. The people embarking on this practice typically leave from Merimada (Western Delta). Falcotosian merchants take a major part in this practice due to their influence starting hundreds of years ago. 
> [!translation] Balu is Sumerian for “Without.”
