# Religious
The following are organizations primarily dedicated to praising various [[Deities]] around the world.

## Temple of the Twin Goddesses
To the Temple of the Twin Goddesses, love and life are two sides of the same coin, and one leads to another. A world of love is a world that nurtures. The temple praises [[Deities#Ianna, Goddess of Love and War|The Lioness]] and [[Deities#Nilvantra, Goddess of Life and Knowledg|Cultivation]] as two sides of the same coin, and revere the god-king [[Deities#Sarru Zalalahara|Sarru Zalalahara]] as their husband. A series of divinely appointed priestesses and their servants run the temple.

While it is the State Religion in the Delta and plays a major role in the life of [[Deltan]] individuals, the Temple of the Twin Goddesses has locations open across the globe where they conduct research on a variety of biomes, provide marriage counseling, and other acts of love in exchange for a donation. 

They also offer free testing for illicit substances at their sites. These temples are operated by divinely appointed Priestesses and the servants that care for them. The most popular festival they host is the Fertility Festival across Sanguia.
